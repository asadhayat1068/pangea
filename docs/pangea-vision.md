# Pangea — Vision Brief (v1)

## Mission
To let any business turn on world-class commerce infrastructure — storefront,
payments, logistics, delivery, and mobility — as easily as flipping a switch,
without assembling a stack of vendors.

## Tagline
One platform. Every commerce experience.

## The problem
Running a modern commerce business means stitching together a dozen vendors: a
storefront tool, a payment processor, a delivery network, a logistics provider,
a dispatch system. Each integration is slow, brittle, and expensive. Small and
mid-size businesses cannot get the capabilities the giants have.

## The wedge (why marketplace first)
We start with the smallest complete commerce loop — list a product, put it in a
cart, check out, fulfil an order — for one real tenant (FreshCart). Every later
capability (logistics, food delivery, payments, ride-sharing, multi-tenant SaaS,
platform APIs, global scale, enterprise) is switched on from this same platform.

## The customer (now)
FreshCart — an online grocer currently selling via spreadsheets and WhatsApp.

## What success looks like in 12 months
A single multi-tenant platform where a business signs up, switches on only the
capabilities it needs, and is taking real orders — with a second and third tenant
live on the same codebase, no rewrite.

## North-Star metric
Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) processed across all tenants.

## Release-1 proxy metric
First real (mock-paid) order created and fulfilled for FreshCart.

## Out of scope right now
Payments integration, search, recommendations, mobile apps, multi-region — all
arrive in named later releases, not now.
